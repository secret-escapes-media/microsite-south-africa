South Africa is a veritable playground for the outdoor types – whether you’re an explorer or a thrill seeker, this epic landscape caters to all.

subjects: - title: Explorer content: |

Hogsback, Eastern Cape

Explore the luscious and beautiful Hogsback region, for endless trails through forests, interspersed with incredible waterfalls. This hippie haven is a paradise for outdoor lovers, with stunning scenery and plenty of animals to spot. The Amathole Horse Trails offer a great way to see the area, on the back of your own steed, whilst mountain biking is also on offer.

Sea kayaking in the Western Cape

The Atlantic coastline of the Western Cape is ripe for exploration. With a variety of epic backdrops, from cityscapes to rolling mountains and sheer cliffs, a calm kayak along the coastline is a great way to explore this usually inaccessible coast.

Table Mountain Cable Car

If you love the sublime views South Africa offers, then don’t miss the chance to saunter effortlessly up to Table Mountain in a cable car. The views can be enjoyed in full panorama, as the car slowly eases its way to the top. An unmissable experience that’s far easier than hiking!

Soweto Bungee jump vs. Bloukrans Bridge Bungee jump

There’s no shortage of opportunity for jumping off great heights and hoping that the elastic band stopping you from falling to the ground is attached at both ends. If that’s your kind of thrill, then you have an impeccable choice in-front of you. Bungee between the iconic Soweto Orlando Towers, or tackle the highest commercial bridge bungee in the world, Bloukrans Bridge in the Eastern Cape – the choice is yours, but one thing is guaranteed, a rush of fear and adrenaline like no other!

Shark Cage Diving

Depending on your feeling towards these fascinating fish, this experience is equal parts adrenaline-fuelled and simply stunning. Jaws flashbacks aside, you’ll be able to get up close and personal with the great white sharks in the area. The cages are not fully submerged, meaning you don’t need diving experience to enjoy this thrilling natural encounter.

Oribi Gorge Adventures

Forget the Big Five, here you’ll find the adrenaline fuelled Wild Five. This one stop shop for all things adventurous is a thrill seeker's paradise nestled in some stunning South African wilderness. Go white water rafting, or abseil next to the Lehrs Falls. Adrenaline meets sublime landscapes, encapsulated in the Wild Swing – the highest and most popular swing in South Africa which will see you leap from the top of a waterfall into the vast gorge below.
